# Flagship Product Roadmap

This repo should not become a pile of average chatbots. The target is a focused AI automation platform that produces a small number of premium, demo-ready products.

## 2026 Focus

Build five flagship product families and improve the generator around them.

| Priority | Flagship | Goal |
| ---: | --- | --- |
| 1 | SourceProof AI RAG Answer Auditor | Audit source-grounded answers and citation quality |
| 2 | ClaimGuard AI Revenue Integrity Copilot | Review medical coding and claim documentation risks |
| 3 | BioSignal AI Paper-to-Decision Analyst | Convert biotech papers into diligence-ready decisions |
| 4 | OpsPilot AI Workflow Risk Commander | Turn business processes into automation plans |
| 5 | ModelBench AI Local LLM Evaluation Console | Score local model outputs for private AI deployment |

## Product Quality Targets

Each flagship should have:

- live demo
- screenshot
- sample data
- product spec
- clear ICP
- real workflow beyond generic chat
- API route with OpenAI SDK
- pricing hypothesis of $1000/month or more
- measurable quality score
